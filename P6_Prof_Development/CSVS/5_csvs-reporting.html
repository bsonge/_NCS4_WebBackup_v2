CSVS Customized Reporting

An organization’s authorized administrator(s) have access to on-demand reporting to verify their participant(s) training certification. Authorized administrators may request the following reports about whether or not their active participants have:

  • Completed their assigned certification
  • Not completed their assigned certification
  • Accessed the training modules and when
  • Completed venue-specific training

Authorized administrators may request other types of reports. Some may incur additional costs depending on the nature of request.

Organization’s authorized administrator(s) have a dashboard they can customize based on their data and analytic needs. Its quick-look format presents authorized administrator(s) relevant and immediately useful status information about their enrolled applicants and participants.
