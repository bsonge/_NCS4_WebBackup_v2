What are the benefits of certification?

The CSSP certification program is ideal for individuals who wish to distinguish themselves among other professionals, show evidence of advanced industry knowledge, and prepare for career advancement. Certification is documented evidence of expertise and experience which can help you gain professional credibility in the field of sports safety and security. Earning a professional certification demonstrates a high level of dedication to the advancement of the sports safety and security profession and raises the bar for industry standards. The CSSP designation reflects a deep personal commitment and sense of accountability that inspires credibility and confidence in your professional knowledge.

Paul Turner, CFE CSSP, shares his outlook on the benefits of certification

The CSSP credential can provide you with:

  • Increased credibility and confidence in your professional knowledge
  • Demonstrated commitment to professional growth and development
  • Enhanced employment opportunities
  • Expanded network of industry peers
  • Recognition as a certified industry professional
